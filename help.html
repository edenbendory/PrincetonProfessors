Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: Where can I submit a new review?
A: To submit a new review, click on the Review a Professor button on the top of this screen. Alternatively, click here.
Q: I'm lost. How can I get back to the home page?
A: You can always click on the words 'Princeton Professors' in the top left corner of the screen to navigate back to the home page.
Q: I regret submitting a review and want to take it down. How can I do this?
A: Email any of the admins listed on this page for assistance with removing a review.
Q: How do you assess professors?
A: Professors are scored on quality of content, delivery, availability, and organization. Content refers to choices that a professor makes regarding what material to include in the curriculum for a course. Delivery refers to the professor's ability to clearly explain concepts to students. Availability refers to a professor's willingness to help students with material outside of lecture, and organization refers to the overall clarity in a professor's teaching materials and the planning which went into producing them.
